# ⚖️ Project Description: Lawyer Portfolio Website  

**Project Title:** *Innocent [Surname], Attorney-at-Law – Professional Portfolio*  

**Objective:**  
Create a **multi‑page, professional, mobile‑first website** for a lawyer that communicates **trust, authority, and accessibility**. The site must be visually mature, law‑centered, and strategically designed to convert visitors into clients.  

---

## 🌐 Site Structure (Multi‑Page)
1. **Home Page**  
   - Hero section with lawyer’s name, title, and portrait.  
   - Strong headline (e.g., *“Dedicated to Justice. Committed to You.”*).  
   - Primary CTA: *Contact Me*.  
   - Secondary CTA: *Request CV on WhatsApp* (deep link with prefilled message).  
   - Trust strip with affiliations/bar admissions.  
   - Overview of practice areas (grid of cards).  
   - Featured case results/testimonials.  

2. **About Page**  
   - Professional biography (education, bar admission, years of practice).  
   - Philosophy and values (integrity, advocacy, client focus).  
   - Professional portrait and timeline of career milestones.  

3. **Case Studies Page**  
   - Grid/list of case summaries with outcomes (confidentiality respected).  
   - Each case card: area of law, short description, result, year.  
   - Disclaimer: *“Past results do not guarantee future outcomes.”*  
   - Optional filters (by practice area).  

4. **Contact Page**  
   - Two‑column layout:  
     - Left: Contact form (Name, Email, Message, Consent checkbox).  
     - Right: Direct links (phone, email, WhatsApp CV deep link, office address, hours).  
   - Optional embedded map.  
   - Clear privacy note.  

---

## 🎨 Design & Branding
- **Theme:** White, clean, professional with a **mature navy–charcoal gradient** and subtle **gold accents**.  
- **Typography:** Serif for headings (authority), sans‑serif for body (modern readability).  
- **Imagery:**  
  - Lawyer’s portrait in hero and About page.  
  - Justice icon subtly integrated in Practice Areas.  
  - Affiliation logos in grayscale trust strip.  
  - No cliché gavels or handshake stock photos.  

---

## 🛠 Technical Requirements
- **HTML5 + CSS (Grid + Flexbox)** for layout.  
- **CSS Tokens** (predefined variables for colors, spacing, typography, shadows, gradients).  
- **Responsive:** Mobile‑first, breakpoints at 768px (tablet) and 1024px (desktop).  
- **Accessibility:** Semantic landmarks, alt text, ARIA labels, visible focus states, WCAG AA contrast.  
- **Performance:** Optimized images, lazy‑loading, font‑display: swap, minimal JS.  
- **SEO:** Meta titles/descriptions, Open Graph tags, schema (Organization, LegalService, Person).  

---

## 🔗 WhatsApp CV Deep Link
- **Format:**  
  `https://wa.me/{phone}?text=Hello%20{LawyerName}%2C%20can%20I%20have%20your%20CV%3F`  
- **Placement:** Header utility button, Hero secondary CTA, Footer quick link.  
- **Label:** *“Request CV on WhatsApp”*.  

---

## ✅ Success Criteria
- Professional, law‑centered aesthetic.  
- Fully responsive and accessible.  
- Clear navigation across **Home, About, Case Studies, Contact**.  
- Prominent CTAs (Contact + WhatsApp CV).  
- Strategic imagery and trust signals.  
- Clean, maintainable code using tokens.  
- Fast load, SEO‑ready, and client‑converting.
