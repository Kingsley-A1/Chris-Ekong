# Additional Pages Experience Blueprint

## 404 Page

- Minimal hero with illustration depicting navigation assistance; headline: "Let's get you back on track".
- Provide two CTA buttons: `Return Home` (primary) and `Contact Us` (secondary).
- Include quick links grid to top services and testimonials.
- Add search input to help locate content directly.

## Sitemap / Legal Pages

- Multi-column layout with sections for About, Services, Case Results, Resources, Contact.
- Typography mirrors global body styling with generous spacing for readability.
- For legal terms, use accordions for major sections to keep scanning manageable; provide anchor navigation at top.
