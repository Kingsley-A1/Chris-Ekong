# About Page Experience Blueprint

## Mission Introduction

- Layout: split grid with portrait occupying columns 1-5 and text block columns 7-12; overlapping quick facts badge anchored at column 6.
- Content: hero heading reiterating mission, supporting paragraph, bullet list of core values, CTA linking to contact page.
- Quick facts badge: includes years practicing, bar admission, and languages served.
- Mobile: portrait becomes full-width top image with quick facts overlay; text follows.

## Career Timeline

- Horizontal scroller using snap points; milestone cards show year, title, brief description, and relevant icon.
- Parallax effect: background diagonal band shifts at slower speed for depth.
- Accessibility: provide alternative vertical list for screen readers and reduced-motion preference.

## Credentials Grid

- 2x3 card grid displaying certifications, professional associations, and awards.
- Each card: emblem icon, credential name, short descriptor.
- Hover state reveals `View Credential` link for supporting documentation.
- Mobile: stack cards in single column with alternating background tints.

## Team and Partners Section

- Modular cards enabling future additions; currently include Barr Chris card plus placeholders for strategic partners.
- Card structure: portrait, name, role, specialty bullet, and `Connect` button.
- Optionally integrate hover reveal for bio excerpt; on mobile, tap expands accordion.
