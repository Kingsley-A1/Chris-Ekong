About Barr. Chris Ekong & Co.

A well-respected, noble and reliable professional lawyer based in Calabar, Cross River State. Practicing in civil litigation, commercial law and family law with a client-centered approach.

With over a decade of experience, Barr. Chris has established a reputation for providing exceptional legal counsel and representation. His dedication to his clients and his unwavering commitment to justice are the cornerstones of his practice.
